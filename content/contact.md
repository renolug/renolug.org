The best way to get involved is to show up and say hello — online or in person.
Pick whichever channel suits you:

## Chat & discussion

- **Mailing list** — our longest-running home base.
  [groups.google.com/g/renolug](https://groups.google.com/g/renolug)
- **Signal group** — real-time chat for quick questions and coordination.
  [Join the group](/signal)

## Follow along

- **Bluesky** — announcements and updates.
  [@renolug.org](https://bsky.app/profile/renolug.org)

## Build with us

- **GitHub** — this website is open source, and so is the group. Issues and
  pull requests welcome. [github.com/renolug](https://github.com/renolug)

## Email

Prefer email? Reach out to us at
[brian@ciq.com](mailto:brian@ciq.com) for now. (A dedicated group address is on
the way.)

## Want to help?

We're just getting going, which means there's plenty to do and lots of room to
shape what RLUG becomes. Whether you want to give a talk, host a meeting, help
with the website, or just spread the word — say hi on the mailing list or in
the Signal group. Everyone's welcome.
